Row, Pull & Core Survival

              4 Rounds · 4:00 On / 1:00 Off
            

            
4 Rounds — 4:00 On / 1:00 Off
300 m Row
10 Deadlifts (185 lb)
10 Burpees Over Bar
10 Pull-ups
Max Toes-to-Bar in remaining time

            

              

                
Rule

                
DNF if you fail to complete the required work in any round.

              

              

                
My result

                
23 total Toes-to-Bar
(5 reps each round × 4 rounds, plus extra reps)

              

              

                
Difficulty

                
Advanced

              

              

                
Focus

                
Rowing, posterior chain, gymnastics pulling, core endurance

              

              

                
Why I recommend it

                
This WOD forces you to manage pacing under a strict time window. The deadlift and burpee combination raises fatigue quickly, while the pull-ups and max T2B test grip and midline endurance.

              

              

                
Scaling tips

                

                  
    
                    
  • Reduce deadlift load to 135/95 lb
    • 
                    
  • Use jumping pull-ups or ring rows
    • 
                    
  • Replace T2B with hanging knee raises
    • 
                    
  • Lower the row distance to 250 m if needed
    • 
                  

                

              

            

          


          
          

            

              
Front Squat Engine Test

              4 Rounds · Every 5:00
            

            
4 Rounds — Every 5:00
9 cal Row
10 Front Squats (115 lb)
10 Burpees Over Bar
5 Thrusters (115 lb)
12 cal Row

            

              

                
Target

                
Under 3:00 each round

              

              

                
Time cap

                
4:00 each round

              

              

                
My result

                
[YOUR RESULT — e.g. 4 rounds under 3:00]

              

              

                
Difficulty

                
Intermediate to Advanced

              

              

                
Focus

                
Leg stamina, barbell cycling, rowing capacity, breathing control

              

              

                
Why I recommend it

                
This WOD is a great test of barbell stamina and aerobic recovery. The front squat and thruster pairing creates heavy leg fatigue, while the two rowing segments force athletes to control breathing before and after barbell work.

              

              

                
Scaling tips

                

                  
    
                    
  • Reduce barbell load to 95/65 lb or 75/55 lb
    • 
                    
  • Reduce burpees to 6–8 reps
    • 
                    
  • Reduce row calories if the target time is unrealistic
    • 
                    
  • Keep at least 60 seconds of rest each round if possible
    •
