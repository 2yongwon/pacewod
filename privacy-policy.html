Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

PaceWOD ("we," "us," or "our") operates https://pacewod.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website and use our free running and CrossFit tools.

Information We Collect

Our calculators and tools run entirely in your browser. We do not require account registration, and we do not store the numbers you enter (pace, heart rate, weight, etc.) on our servers.

We may automatically receive certain information through standard web technologies, including:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs
  • General geographic location (country/city level) derived from IP address
  • Cookie and similar tracking data as described below

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (Google Tag: G-MV7V4N6TQV) to understand how visitors find and use PaceWOD. Google Analytics collects aggregated usage data such as page views and session duration. You can learn how Google processes this data in Google's Privacy Policy and opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve site functionality and to serve advertisements through Google AdSense (Publisher ID: ca-pub-9575554196184772). Google and its advertising partners may use cookies to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to PaceWOD and other websites
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraud
  • Limit how often you see the same advertisement

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads on PaceWOD. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet.

  • Opt out of personalized advertising: Google Ads Settings
  • Opt out of third-party vendor cookies: aboutads.info (US) or youronlinechoices.eu (EU)

For more information on how Google uses data from partner sites, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Google AdSense

AdSense helps us keep PaceWOD's tools and guides free. Advertisers may collect information about your interactions with ads in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Users in the European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland: where required by law, we work with Google to display consent messaging for personalized ads. You can change your ad personalization preferences at any time through the links above.

California residents may request information about categories of personal information we collect and ask us to delete personal information we hold, subject to legal exceptions. To make a privacy request, email yong_93@naver.com.

Our site contains links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

PaceWOD is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect information transmitted to our site. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
