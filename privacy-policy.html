Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

PaceWOD ("we," "us," or "our") operates https://pacewod.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website and use our free running and CrossFit tools.

Information We Collect

Our calculators and tools run entirely in your browser. We do not require account registration, and we do not store the numbers you enter (pace, heart rate, weight, etc.) on our servers.

We may automatically receive certain information through standard web technologies, including:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs
  • General geographic location (country/city level) derived from IP address
  • Cookie and similar tracking data as described below

Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve site functionality and to serve advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to PaceWOD and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor cookies by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Google AdSense

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads on PaceWOD. AdSense helps us keep our tools free for athletes worldwide. Advertisers may collect information about your interactions with ads in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Analytics

We may use Google Search Console and similar services to understand how visitors find and use our site. These services provide aggregated, non-personally-identifiable statistics.

Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

PaceWOD is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect information transmitted to our site. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
